Trip log analysis (for trips completed through 2023)

The trip log is a list of all of my outdoor adventure trips. I made the list in February 2024 using the timestamps of my trip photos that I had previously organized by trip location and year. The log provides a plethora of information that I thought would be interesting to analyze. For example, How many nights have I spent in the backcountry? Who have I traveled with the most?, Where have I traveled the most?, and so on. My results from the analysis are listed below. The analysis was completed using trip log entries dated through 2023.

Total trips: 87

Total days spent on trips: 278

Average trip length: 3 days

Average number of trips per year (2016 - 2023): 9.25

Average number of days spent on trips per year (2016 - 2023): 29

Most frequent travel companions: David Krawitz (24 trips, 65 days), Michael McGill (19 trips, 68 days)
