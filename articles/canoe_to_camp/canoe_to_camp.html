Canoeing to camp

Canoeing is the most elegant form of wilderness travel. There is no trail or path left behind as one stalks wildlife and remote campsites. While the design is simple, canoes require technical prowess to safely reach one’s destination. Any rapid quickly turns dicey when weighted down with camping gear and drinks. Teamwork is key. The places traveled by canoe are inherently remote and provide great fishing opportunities. If one seeks a new way to experience camping, they should surely get out canoeing.
