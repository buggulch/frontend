Prewitt Ridge, Big Sur - Car Camping

Location: Prewitt Ridge, Big Sur
Type of trip: Car Camping
Date: Feb. 6-7, 2016
People: Sara Wilson, Fiona Jackson, Nicole, Natalie Cox, Jack Christy, Sid Maurer, Drew Taylor, Chris Farkas, David Krawitz

This was the monumental first camping trip of freshman year. Ten of us dormmates cruised up Highway 1 on a sunny winter weekend in February. At a cliffside putout, we spotted a pack of migrating gray whales directly below us. This was and still is by far the best whale sighting I have ever seen.

We then made our way up Nacimiento Fergusson Road and onto the dirt road that leads to Prewitt Ridge. Fiona successfully navigated the road with her Lexus sedan. We settled on one of the many ocean view campsites along the ridge. A disgusting amount of photos were taken during sunset that evening.

The next day we hiked up to the Big Sur River Gorge. Upon our arrival, we spooked a large steelhead. The water was insanely cold but refreshing. The same went for Salmon Creek Falls, our last stop of the weekend.
