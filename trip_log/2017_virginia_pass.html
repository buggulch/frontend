Virginia Pass Loop, Hoover Wilderness - Backpacking

Location: Virginia Pass Loop, Hoover Wilderness
Type of trip: Backpacking
Date: Aug. 2-4, 2017
People: Matt Lane, Michael McGill

This was an amazing loop out of Green Creek Trailhead. Snow pack from the infamous 2017 winter lingered throughout the mountains and resulted in a harrowing traverse of a large snowfield leading up to Virginia Pass. Michael spotted a heard of Sierra Nevada Bighorn Sheep, an extremely rare sighting. A ranger passed by while we were viewing and was also stunned to see the goats. At the time of the sighting, the Sierra Nevada Big Horn Sheep population was around 600. Our last night was spent at East Lake. Rain forced us to finish off our drinks inside our tents. I caught a nice brown trout moments before we hiked out the next morning. The first mile of the hike out had the worst mosquitoes I had ever experienced.
