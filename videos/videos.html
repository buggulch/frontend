Bay area waterfalls

Storm after storm, waterfall after waterfall. 2023's record setting winter did not disappoint.

With travel to the mountains frequently barred by snowstorms, friends and I ventured out to local canyons in search of waterfalls. Not only did we find several amazing cascades, but also discovered that waterfall hunting is one of the best ways to explore nature. A rainy day hike up a hidden gully can easily beat a blue bird power day on the mountain.

Plan your visit!

Redding to Red Bluff

Good friends Mason, Sid, and Preston traveled 2500 miles across the country to experience California’s beautiful high alpine terrain. The east coasters were welcomed with a surge of cold weather, rain, and smoke. This triplex unexpectedly forced the boys from the mountains to the flatlands of Redding. While not as trendy as their home, Redding offers a wonderful float down California’s largest river. So the ensemble took to the water with 6 of their finest mates and unleashed their inner Jack Sparrow.
